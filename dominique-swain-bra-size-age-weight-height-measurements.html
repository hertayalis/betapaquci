    Biography:

    Dominique Swain was born on August 12, 1980 in Malibu, California, USA. She first gained recognition for her portrayal of Jamie Archer in the 1997 movie Face/Off. That same year, she won the title role in the 1997 remake of Lolita.

    For her role as Lolita, she won the Young Artist Awards for Best Performance in a TV Movie / Pilot / Mini-Series: Leading Young Actress. She then appeared as Andrea Marr in the film Girl in 1998.

    She has since starred in other films including Dead in the Water, Alpha Dog, Devour, Road to Nowhere, Toxic, Blue Dream and many others.
