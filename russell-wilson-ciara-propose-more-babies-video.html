    See Hilarious Video of Russell Wilson Asking Ciara for More Babies

    Russell Wilson is ready to expand his family with Ciara. Last week, while the Grammy-winning singer guest-hosted an episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show, her Seahawks quarterback husband joined her on set to pop a special question.

    30 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Russell Wilson is ready to expand his family with Ciara.

    Last week, while the Grammy-winning singer guest-hosted an episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show, her Seahawks quarterback husband joined her on set to pop a special question.

    "The funny thing is that Russ makes me really nervous," Ciara explained to the audience, holding a big bouquet of roses Wilson gave her.

    "Well, I do know everything about you, first of all," Wilson replied. "And second of all, I want to make you more nervous right now." He then dropped to one knee. "I have a question for you, a serious question. Can we have more babies? I mean, it would be perfect. Just give me one more, at least."

    Ciara laughed before answering, "We definitely can, but we've got a little time before we get there."

    The couple welcomed their second child together, a baby boy named Win Harrison Wilson, in July 2020. They share a daughter, Sienna Princess Wilson, who was born in April 2017. Ciara also shares seven-year-old Future Zahir Wilburn with her ex, rapper Future.

    Of choosing Win's name, Ciara told Entertainment Tonight, "That is Daddy. I give credit to Dad. ... Russell would always be like, 'Here's Win shortstop,' he like, plays a whole thing out. And then obviously, Harrison, it was his dad's first name, which is really sweet and precious."

    She added that the two had been thinking of different baby names for years. "There was a lot of love and thought put into it," the singer continued. "Russ, we would talk about names, and Russ kind of always had this name Win in the rotation, years ago, before we knew we were having a girl. He had all the good names and Win won. So Win is the name and he's so cute, he's so precious."
