    Doyoung Bio, Age, Height, Parents, Siblings, Net Worth – Doyoung is a versatile South Korean artist involved in singing, acting, and hosting. As a prominent member of the South Korean boy group NCT, he contributes to various sub-units such as NCT U, NCT 127, and the newly formed NCT DoJaeJung.

    Doyoung Biography

    Doyoung embarked on his journey in the entertainment industry by joining SM Entertainment as a trainee in late 2013, following his discovery at the Guri City Youth Art Festival singing competition.

    His career began gaining momentum when he participated in Exo member Chanyeol’s music video remake of H.O.T’s song “Hope” on the TV series “Exo 90:2014” in 2014. Later, in January 2015, he was introduced as a member of SM Rookies and co-hosted MBC Music’s music show, “Show Champion,” with fellow member Jaehyun.

    His talent further shone when he appeared in the Korean remake of “The Mickey Mouse Club” in 2015, performing a duet with fellow SM Rookies member Koeun. In February 2016, he became a model for the Design United brand alongside Taeyong and Yuta.

    From 2016 to 2018, his career soared as he became part of NCT U, NCT 127, and participated in various projects such as “Lipstick Prince” and “Sound of Your Heart.” He also joined “Inkigayo” as a host in February 2017 and appeared on “We Got Married” alongside his brother in March of the same year.

    He continued to make waves in the industry with his involvement in soundtracks, collaborations, and re-recordings. In March 2019, he collaborated with indie duo Rocoberry on the song “Don’t Say Goodbye.” He also participated in the popular show “King of Mask Singer” in July 2019.

    In September 2020, he played a pivotal role in NCT’s Resonance album series. His talents extended to acting as he starred in “Midnight Cafe – The Curious Stalker” in March 2021 and made his musical debut in “Marie Antoinette.”

    2023 marked the debut of the NCT DoJaeJung sub-unit, featuring Doyoung along with Jaehyun and Jungwoo. He also secured the role of brand ambassador for Dolce & Gabbana in Korea and Japan. Additionally, Doyoung released the soundtrack song “Beautiful Day” for the third season of the TV series “Dr. Romantic.”

    Beyond his entertainment career, he has displayed his philanthropic side. In March 2022, he donated ₩30 million to the Hope Bridge Disaster Relief Association, aiding wildfire victims. In February 2023, he contributed ₩100 million to the Community Chest of Korea to support victims of the 2023 Turkey–Syria earthquake.

    His multifaceted talents and dedication to philanthropy have solidified his status as a well-rounded and impactful artist in the Korean entertainment industry.
