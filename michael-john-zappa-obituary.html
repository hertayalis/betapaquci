    Obituary and memorial information for Michael John Zappa

    Remembering the life of Michael John Zappa

    It is with great sadness that we share the news of the passing of Michael John Zappa, a beloved resident of Wood Dale, Illinois. Michael’s presence touched the lives of many, including his devoted parents, Eugene and Cecelia, and his loving partner, Mary. He was loved and appreciated by those who knew him. Today, we gather to remember him and honor his influence on our lives.

    The passing of Michael John Zappa

    Farewell words to a remarkable individual

    We bid farewell to Michael John Zappa, a remarkable individual who left an indelible mark on our hearts. His passing left a void that can never be filled. Michael’s kindness, compassion and zest for life will forever be remembered by all who had the privilege of knowing him. As we mourn his loss, let us also celebrate the beautiful moments we shared with him and the legacy he left behind.

    Condolences and remembrance page

    Share our deepest condolences

    During this sad time, we extend our deepest condolences to the Zappa family. We invite you to join us in expressing your sympathy and sharing your fondest memories of Michael on the memorial page dedicated to his life. Through our collective support and remembrance, we can bring comfort and strength to his loved ones during this difficult time. Let us come together as a community to honor and celebrate the life of a truly remarkable individual.

    Details of the memorial service

    A time to remember and reflect

    We respectfully invite you to join us at this memorial service honoring the life of Michael John Zappa. The service will take place on Saturday, December 30, 2023, from 2:00 pm to 5:00 pm at Geils Funeral Home, located at 260 W Irving Park Rd, Wood Dale, Illinois 60191. This service will create an opportunity for friends, family and loved ones to come together to share stories and pay tribute to the extraordinary man that Michael was. Your presence and support will mean so much to us as we remember and honor his life.

    Donate in lieu of flowers

    Supporting causes close to Michael’s heart

    In lieu of flowers, the Zappa family kindly requests donations be made to two organizations that have a special place in Michael’s heart: PAWS Chicago and Hinsdale Humane Society. By donating to these causes, we can honor Michael’s love of animals and continue his legacy of compassion. Your generosity will make a meaningful difference in the lives of people in need, just as Michael did during his time with us.
