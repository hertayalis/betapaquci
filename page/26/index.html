Apex to open new satellite factory in Los Angeles

23 Aug 2024, 00:00

WASHINGTON — Satellite manufacturer Apex will open a new factory next year to ramp up production of small satellites. The company announced Dec. 14 it signed a lease for a nearly 4,300-square-meter building in the Playa Vista neighborhood of Los Angeles that will be a production facility it calls Factory One. Development of the facility is supported by a $16 million Series A round the company raised in June. The factory, projected to open in the third quarter of 2024, will be able to produce up to 50 satellites a year, said Chief Executive Ian Cinnamon in an interview.
