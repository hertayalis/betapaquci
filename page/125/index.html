Answer It should be noted that the majority of quartzites that etch are mislabeled. Quartzite, in its natural state, is very durable. Even while etching is more obvious on light/white coloured stone, the dulling look of etching will be seen on any hue quartzite. People have also inquired as to whether quartzite can be leathered. Compared to polished quartzite, polished quartzite does not display etching as quickly; nevertheless, the honing process widens the pores, making the stone more prone to discoloration.
