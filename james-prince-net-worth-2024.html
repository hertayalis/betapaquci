    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    James Prince was born and raised in Houston, Texas. He started his career in the music industry in the mid-1980s when he founded Rap-A-Lot Records. The label is known for its contribution to the popularization of the Dirty South style of rap music. The success of Rap-A-Lot Records has been a significant contributor to Prince’s wealth.
