    How did Shefali Jariwala start her Professional Career?

    Shefali Jariwala made her acting debut in the “Kaanta Laga” music video in 2002. Following the release of the music video, Shefali experienced an overnight phenomenon due to the song’s success.     Her other music videos include “Maal Bhari Aahe – DJ Hot Remix,” “Kabhi Aar Kabhi Paar Remix,” and others. She made a fleeting appearance in the 2004 movie “Mujhse Shaadi Karoge.” The dancer appeared in the music video for “Pyar Humaayn Kis Mod Pe Le Aaya” by Meet Bros in 2006. She also made an appearance in the song “Naa Board Irada Bus Pankaja” from the movie “Hudugaru.”
