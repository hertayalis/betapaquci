    Glenn Villeneuve (born August 18, 1969) is an American Reality TV Star, Survivalist, YouTube Content Creator, and Social Media Personality. He first rose to fame after starring in the documentary television series “Life Below Zero”.

    The show depicts the daily activities and experiences of hunters. They need to live in remote areas of Alaska. Furthermore, the program came out on the National Geographic Channel on May 19, 2013.

    In the show, Villeneuve resides alone in Chandalar 200 miles north of Fairbanks, Alaska. The place is also 65 miles North of the Arctic Circle. The other main casts of the series were Chip and Agnes Hailstone, Andy Bassich, Ricko DeWilde, Erik and Martha Salitan, Sue Aikens, and Jessie Holmes.

    Life Below Zero even bagged awards like 68th Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Awards, 69th Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Awards, 70th Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Awards, and 71st Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Awards.

    In 2019, the TV personality even appeared in the 11h season of the show which ended on February 19, 2019. His fans became sad when he announced that he will not star in the 12th season of the series via Facebook.
